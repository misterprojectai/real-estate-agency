# Buyer Transaction Checklist

last_updated: 2025-05-14
version: 1.0
jurisdiction: Texas residential — TREC contracts
used_by: 04_transaction_coordinator (buyer deals only)

---

When creating a Deal File, TC copies applicable items from this checklist
and tracks completion status. Note this version in the Deal File.

Update this file and increment version when TREC requirements change or
Austin market practice shifts. Active deals retain the version they were
created under.

---

## Pre-contract

- [ ] Buyer pre-approval letter on file
- [ ] Lender contact confirmed
- [ ] Buyer agency agreement signed

## At contract execution

- [ ] TREC One to Four Family Residential Contract — fully executed
- [ ] Earnest money delivered to title company — receipt confirmed
- [ ] Option fee delivered to seller — receipt confirmed
- [ ] Third Party Financing Addendum — signed (if financed)
- [ ] Addendum for Property Subject to Mandatory Membership in HOA — signed (if HOA)
- [ ] Seller's Disclosure Notice received and reviewed
- [ ] Lead-based paint disclosure — signed (if pre-1978 construction)
- [ ] Any additional addenda identified and signed

## Option period

- [ ] Home inspection scheduled
- [ ] Home inspection completed
- [ ] Inspection report received
- [ ] Repair amendment or termination decision made
- [ ] Amendment to Contract signed (if repairs negotiated)
- [ ] Option period termination or continuation confirmed

## Title and survey

- [ ] Title commitment received
- [ ] Title commitment reviewed — no unacceptable exceptions
- [ ] Survey ordered (or existing survey located)
- [ ] T-47 Residential Real Property Affidavit signed (if using existing survey)
- [ ] Survey received and reviewed
- [ ] Survey approved by lender (if applicable)

## HOA (if applicable)

- [ ] HOA resale certificate ordered
- [ ] HOA resale certificate received within deadline
- [ ] HOA financials reviewed — no material concerns
- [ ] HOA rules and regulations reviewed
- [ ] HOA transfer documentation initiated

## Financing

- [ ] Loan application submitted
- [ ] Appraisal ordered
- [ ] Appraisal received
- [ ] Appraisal at or above contract price (or amendment negotiated)
- [ ] Loan approval / commitment letter received
- [ ] Financing contingency satisfied or waived
- [ ] Homeowner's insurance binder obtained

## Pre-closing

- [ ] Final walkthrough scheduled
- [ ] Final walkthrough completed — no undisclosed changes
- [ ] Closing Disclosure received (3 business days before closing)
- [ ] Closing Disclosure reviewed — figures match expectations
- [ ] Wire transfer instructions received from title
- [ ] Wire instructions verified via phone call to title (NOT email alone)
- [ ] Funds wired to title
- [ ] Wire confirmed received by title company

## Closing

- [ ] ALTA Settlement Statement reviewed before signing
- [ ] All documents signed
- [ ] Deed recorded — confirmation obtained
- [ ] Title policy issued
- [ ] Keys, garage remotes, and access codes received
- [ ] Utility transfers confirmed

## Post-close

- [ ] Client follow-up sent
- [ ] Lead Card status updated to closed
- [ ] Referral request noted in activity log
